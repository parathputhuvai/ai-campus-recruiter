Frequently Asked Questions

Our AI-driven automatic test generation and evaluation system eliminates bias while providing real-time insights. Unlike traditional platforms, we offer adaptive difficulty levels and intelligent candidate comparison features.

Absolutely! Recruiters can modify questions manually, regenerate specific categories, or accept AI-generated questions. Our platform offers complete flexibility while maintaining AI-powered efficiency.

Candidates log in using their Exam ID combined with the last 3 digits of their Registration Number. This ensures secure, one-time access while maintaining simplicity.

We use enterprise-grade encryption for all data, support 1000+ concurrent users, and complete AI evaluations within 5 seconds. Our security measures ensure complete data protection and fair testing.
